\opening{Dear Professor Levy,}

Enclosed, please find two copies of 
{\em Trace Re-Setting Density Matrix Purification in ${\cal O}(N)$ Self-Consistent-Field Theory}
by Niklasson, Tymczak and Challacombe, which I am submitting for publication in the Journal of Chemical Physics. 
This article presents a superior algorithm which supplants our previous work on ${\cal O}(N)$ density matrix solvers
{\em A simplified density matrix minimization for linear scaling self-consistent field theory}, which appeared
in the Journal of Chemical Physics in 1999.  This work also presents analysis of error accumulation and 
stability for the new method.

I hope you will consider this submission for publication in the Journal of Chemical Physics.
Suggestions for reviewers with expertise in area include:
Martin Head-Gordon at Berkeley (mhg@bastille.cchem.berkeley.edu), 
David Bowler at Imperial College London (david.bowler@ucl.ac.uk),
Stephan Goedecker at CEA France (goedecke@drfmc.ceng.cea.fr),
or Giulia Galli at LLNL (galligygi1@llnl.gov).
With reservation, I also mention Gustavo Scuseria at Rice, with whom conflict of interest may
exist.

\closing{With best regards,}
